Data Processing

  1. Remove auto-created messages (e.g., someone added to a group)
  2. Exclude all messages where party did not sign consent form
  3. Exclude all messages without demographic information
  4. Remove all messages with less than 250 words
  5. Resulting in 199 participants
  6. 1,550.91 messages (SD=3,576.19) with 1,949,518 words, 80,943 emoji, 48,777 emoticons

Discussion

Positives
  • Gender and Age are predictable from messages
  • Random Forest outperforms Elastic Net and SVM
  • Specific personal pronouns, emoji and emoticon are associated with age and gender
  • First study to predict demographics based on WhatsApp data
  • Demonstrated that prediction is better if more words included

Discussion

Negatives
  • Manual, theory-driven approach reduces complexity
  • LIWC has no contextual nor topic understanding
  • Not adjusted to non-canonical language
  • Old data set (with difficult to obtain data)
  • Emoticon/ emoji usage changes by technology and time
  • Mutual influence of participants not captured

Outlook & Future Research

  • Non-parametric, theory-free methodolgy
  • Memetic influence
  • Group dynamics
  • Geospatial Research of Linguistic Features
  • Longitudinal Research of Linguistic Features
